FEST

A college fest plays a very important role in one’s college life. Agreed, college is a place where you build a path for your entire future,but there’s so much more to it.

In college ,it is very important to socialize and to meet the right people . And there’s no better place for this than a college fest.

You deserve a break from your studies.

Fests push you to socialize with people and not be crammed up in your room in all day.

You get to try a variety of activities.
You discover some hidden talents
You might figure out just where your heart lies,or you might take uo a fun hobby.

There are interesting workshop and competition which can help to improve your knowledge.
You can win prizes and meet new people in cities.
But most important you spend time with friends amd makes priceless memories.

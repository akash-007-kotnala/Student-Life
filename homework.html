HOMEWORK

Present Situation Many children today have to spend hours every evening doing homewor.
 
Some people seem to believe that this will help the children in their academic career. 
 Others feel that homework is mostly useless. Many teachers give too much homework, and often teachers do not coordinate the quantity given.

The children do not have free time to relax or play sports. 
 A further point is that unnecessary assignments raise the stress level of the student. 

This can actually lower productivity and performance, instead of raising it.
 However, the main argument against homework is that most of it is just boring practice.

 The students learn nothing new – the teachers just give it because they are expected to. 

I agree that some practice is good, but endless practice of the same material does not lead to improvement.

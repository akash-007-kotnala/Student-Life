NIGHTOUT

"Pulling out all-nighters before exams because studying in the morning is too mainstream".... is the best possible excuse that you can give to sneak out for fun-filled night outs with buddies.

No matter what you night out plans are, you'll surely end it with a cup of chai/coffee in the tiny stall near the college that remains open 24x7. Study rooms which are always free had no place to occupy.

It mean studying the whole night preparing for minors and sleeping only after giving the exam in the morning.

Its is something exciting about studying at night just before exam that gave

you the trill of being in a movie like "MISSION IMPOSSIBLE".

There is always a conflict between heart and brain .

Brain: "12 ghante baad exam hai".
Heart:" Fuck ab toh fail,mujhe toh sabji bechna bhi nhi aata!"
Brain:"*singing* Lashya o har haal mein paana hai"

And finishing syllabus will feel like "BAHUBALI".
